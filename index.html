Publi©ity/GFRY Studio presents:

"A proposal for the development of 10 East Lake."

PUBLI©ITY is a transdisciplinary studio intending to harness multiple intelligences, professional experience and creative energy to create a new local conversation about public space, and explore how to create successful smart city projects involving a range of stakeholders. Seeking to use Technology + Science + Social Interaction + Art + Design + Innovation + Entrepreneurship, to further the professional experience of the studio, and, provide the opportunity for students and faculty to work together, to envision and design livable cities, using Chicago as laboratory.
