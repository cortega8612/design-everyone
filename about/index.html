GFRY PUBLI(C)ITY MISSION STATEMENT We are a group of artists, architects, designers, educators, and administrators who are working to create multifunctional public spaces which will serve as a resource for potential projects. This project will foster collaboration, resource sharing, and ecological awareness through technology, community participation, and play.
